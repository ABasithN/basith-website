What good looks like to me

Brands that respect their audience's intelligence. No shouting. No cheap shots at whoever's sitting across the category.

Copy that sounds like a human wrote it for another human. Ideas that don't need three slides of explanation to land.

Apple has been proving this for decades. Simple campaigns, no jargon, no competitor hate. Just pure conviction in what they make. That's not a template. That's a standard.

I've spent 13 years trying to hold that standard for every brief that came my way. Some I got right. All of them made me better.
